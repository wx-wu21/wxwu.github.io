Projects

Desktop Muon Detector
This project was initiated by Prof. Zhihong Ye in the Department of Physics at Tsinghua University. Wenxuan Wu joined the project from 2024 to 2025, assembled a team, and led the development of the first prototype. The project aims to develop low-cost, portable muon detectors suitable for easy deployment in high schools. As the number of detectors grows, they can form a network to measure muon flux at different locations. The collected data can then be used to analyze the thickness of the local atmosphere, providing insights into weather and climate.
